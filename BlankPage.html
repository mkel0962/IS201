Why Marketing

Here is a list of reasons I want to go into the field of marketing!

Marketing is a perfect combination of:

  1. Analytics
    • Studying demographics to see what type of ads would reach them best
    • Analyzing data to see how many people visit a website everyday, stay on the page, or select certain items
  2. Creativity
    • Using information from research to create social media posts, new products, sales initiatives, or whatever applies to your field of marketing
    • Brainstorming with others about best marketing strategies
  3. Other Business Frameworks
    • Improving websites and advertisements through Information Systems
    • Collaborating with salespeople who work from a strategy point of view
    • Directing all initiatives toward customers just like Human Resources

I love thinking through things in an organized, analytical way, but I also love having more than one right answer and way to do things. I also like other aspects of many of the business programs here at BYU, but Marketing stands out to me the most. Because of this, I am currently pursuing a Marketing Degree at BYU.
